Mathare Street Love project

Stable Health's mission is to impact the health of a target population through dissemination of information on the importance of timely diagnosis, prevention, Evidence-based healthcare practices. To this effect, SHF is reaching out to the community by providing healthcare at t he comfort of the target populations home and its environs

SHF in partnership with EQA - Kitengela organised a medical expo at Riverine Estate to reach people at their homes who are disadvantaged to access hospital services. The expo was conducted on 02/09/2020. Some of the services that were offered at the expo include; doctor's Consultation, free vital checkups(weight, BMI, blood pressure, weight measurement, Height measurement, Blood sugar screening), Optical and Dental checkups
