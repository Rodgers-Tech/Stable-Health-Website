Who we are to Serve the nation

Stable Health foundation(SHF) was incorporated in 2017 as a community based Health canre provider. We make sure our clients receive high quality and affordable heath care. We train and supply expperienced healthcare professionals. Thanks to a partnership with a team of health providers which comprises of of doctors, pharmacists, clinical officers, laboratory officers, nutritionists, nurses among other providers.


Most of the our volunteer opportunities consist of field activities in our various projects in communities. We provide personalized Health care to our service users that make a real postive impact on the daily lives. Our large pool of well trained, dedicated and caring staff ensures quality service is available to our clients throught the year .

Our goals & objectives

Who are in extremely love with eco friendly system.

Objective 1
To conduct frequent community outreaches including mobile clinics to reach out to target groups in interior and unreachable population areas to deliver effective and efficient safe care to our patients.
Objective 2
To deliver high Quality Comprehensive Evidence-Based Patient-Focused (EBPF) care services
Objective 3
To increase the capacity of the medical team to facilitate effective delivery of quality health services to patients.
Objective 4
To establish effective financial protection schemes in anticipation to encouraging easy access to medical care for the less privileged clients.
Objective 5
To facilitate financial inclusion and active partnership in delivery of healthcare services in the target population areas
